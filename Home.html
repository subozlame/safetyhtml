Common Disasters

🌍 Earthquake

Drop, Cover, and Hold On. Stay away from windows and heavy furniture.

🌊 Flood

Move to higher ground immediately and avoid walking through floodwater.

🔥 Fire

Use stairs instead of elevators and leave the building quickly.

⛰️ Landslide

Evacuate the area and stay away from steep slopes during heavy rainfall.

Emergency Preparedness Tips

Emergency Kit

Keep water, food, flashlight, batteries, and first aid supplies ready.

Family Plan

Decide meeting points and emergency contacts in advance.

Stay Informed

Follow weather alerts and official emergency announcements.
